About

Welcome to HealthCart, your online destination for health, wellness, and personal care products. We provide a convenient platform where users can explore a variety of wellness essentials, including vitamins, supplements, fitness products, skincare items, and herbal solutions. Our goal is to create a simple and user-friendly shopping experience that helps customers discover products that support healthier lifestyles and everyday well-being.

  • Identifying the primary demographics purchasing health products online.
  • Assessing the influence of digital peer reviews versus professional medical advice.
  • Understanding the perceived trust and safety consumers associate with digital health marketplaces.
